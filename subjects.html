Grade 9 Subjects

Information about the subjects tackled in the Grade 9 level.

MAPEH

Consists of 4 categories-- Music, Arts, Physical Education, and Health. Revolves around drugs, renaissance era, baroque period, greek theatre, and different sports.

MATHEMATICS

A subject that tackles quadratic equations, quadratic functions, linear equations, linear inequality, angles, quadrilaterals, and trigonometry.

VECL

Helps students grow not just academically, but morally and spiritually. Teaches us about Catholicism and values that a prudent Child of God shall show.

SCIENCE

Consists of 4 branches-- Biology, Chemistry, Earth & Space, and Physics. It teaches us about organisms, elements, volcanoes, weather and climate, and motion.

ICT

Grade 9 students used SketchUp, a 3D modeling software, to learn how to design objects, buildings, and spaces digitally, helping them develop creativity and technical skills.

ENGLISH

A subject that teaches students about different literary pieces, poetry, multiple types of texts, theatre, syllogism, diagrams, writing letters, and addressing envelopes.

ARALING PANLIPUNAN

A subject that tackles everything about economics. Its basic concepts, economic systems, the government, financial institutions, and national economy.

FILIPINO

Naka-pokus sa panitikang asyano, balagtasan ng wika, iba't ibang mga kwento, at Noli Me Tangere na isinulat ni Jose P. Rizal-- ang ating pambansang bayani.

TECHNICAL WRITING

A subject that focuses on writing about technical topics, such as instructions, clauses, job descriptions, brochures, research, and resumes.

HOME NURSING

Teaches students skills and techniques needed to provide care and support to their patients. Covers topics such as nursing procedures, patient assessment, and health management.

COOKERY

Involves the art and science of preparing and cooking food, covering techniques, ingredients, and presentation.

BAKING & PASTRY

Focuses on the preparation and creation of various types of baked goods. Also teaches skills such as covering ingredient selection, mixing, baking, and costing.

COSMETOLOGY

Teaches students basic skills in hair, nail, and skin care. Also focuses on proper hygiene, safety, and correct use of beauty tools.

WEB DEVELOPMENT

Lessons coding languages such as HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Students learn how to structure and design a website, and then make it function.
